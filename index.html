Tri-County Dual Meet Points Tracker

This is a free web app for tracking the points accumulated during dual meets in Tri-County Swimming Pool Association. No login, email, or installation is required. How it works:

  • Track your team's points for each event by checking the appropriate box.
  • Scores for both teams update at the bottom in real-time.
  • Points are saved in the browser for 30 days (details), or until you hit "reset".

A single cookie is stored in your browser to save the results you've checked. It is automatically deleted after 30 days or can be cleared manually using the "Reset" button. This site does not use any tracking.
